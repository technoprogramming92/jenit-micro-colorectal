Post-Surgery Care and Precautions

  1. Diet and Nutrition:Post-surgery, a gradual return to a normal diet is recommended. Initially, a low-residue diet may be necessary to ease the digestive process. Dr. Gandhi provides personalized dietary guidelines to help you regain strength and avoid complications.
  2. Physical Activity:Light physical activity is encouraged to promote healing and prevent complications like blood clots. Dr. Gandhi will guide you on when and how to gradually increase your activity levels.
  3. Monitoring and Follow-Up:Regular follow-up visits are crucial to monitor your recovery and check for any signs of recurrence. Dr. Gandhi will schedule periodic colonoscopies and imaging tests to ensure the cancer has not returned.
  4. Managing Side Effects:Post-surgery side effects, such as changes in bowel habits, fatigue, or emotional stress, are common. Dr. Gandhi’s team provides support and resources to help you manage these challenges and improve your quality of life.
